# Chrome Web Store Listing - Apply Codes Extension

## Short Description (132 characters max)
AI recruiting sidebar for LinkedIn: multi-source enrichment (PDL, Hunter.io), AI chat, candidate evaluation & outreach generation.

## Detailed Description (For Chrome Web Store - Max 16,000 characters)

Apply Codes Extension brings the full power of AI recruiting tools directly to LinkedIn with a beautiful sidebar interface. Evaluate candidates, enrich contact data from multiple sources, and generate personalized outreach - all without leaving LinkedIn.

### Key Features

**Smart Sidebar Interface (Like ContactOut/Lusha)**
A professional sidebar that appears on any LinkedIn profile with instant access to all recruiting tools. Clean, organized sections for AI analysis and contact enrichment.

**Multi-Source Contact Enrichment**
Find candidate contact information from multiple data providers:
- **Nymeria Integration** - Professional email and phone lookup
- **People Data Labs (PDL)** - Rich profile enrichment with emails, phones, social profiles, skills, and work history
- **Hunter.io** - Professional email finder with verification status
- Copy any data field with one click

**AI Candidate Evaluation**
Instantly analyze LinkedIn profiles against your job requirements. Get AI-powered assessments including:
- Match score percentage
- Key strengths identified
- Potential concerns flagged
- Hiring recommendation
- Automatic saving to your project

**AI Chat Assistant**
Ask questions about any candidate directly in the sidebar. Get instant AI-powered insights about:
- Skills assessment
- Experience relevance
- Interview question suggestions
- Comparison with requirements

**Boolean Search Generator with AI Explanation**
Create sophisticated boolean search strings for LinkedIn Recruiter in seconds. New: Click "Explain" to get a visual breakdown of your search showing:
- What each component searches for
- Location targeting logic
- Exclusion filters
- Practical tips

**Outreach Content Generation**
Generate personalized recruiting emails and InMails powered by AI. Messages are tailored to each candidate's profile and experience.

**Project Context System**
Organize your recruiting work by project. All candidate evaluations, searches, and research are automatically saved to your selected project and synced with the Apply Codes web app.

**Web Search (Perplexity Integration)**
Research candidates, companies, salaries, and market intelligence directly from the extension.

**URL Scraping (Firecrawl Integration)**
Import job postings, company pages, or any web content with one click. Perfect for importing job descriptions from career sites.

### How It Works

1. Navigate to any LinkedIn profile
2. Click the "Apply Codes" tab on the right side of the screen
3. Use any of the sidebar tools:
   - Click "Evaluate Candidate" for AI analysis
   - Click "PDL Enrich" or "Hunter.io Email" for contact data
   - Use the AI Chat to ask questions about the candidate
   - Generate personalized outreach messages
